
## Scientific Review Template

1. **Dataset Overview**  
   a. What is the **total size** (hours, segments)?  
   b. What are the **domains** and **scenarios** (meetings, telephone, broadcast, egocentric)?  

2. **Recording Conditions**  
   a. What is the **audio quality** (sampling rate, noise level)?  
   b. What **microphone configurations** are used (single, multichannel, headset, array)?  

3. **Speaker & Language Characteristics**  
   a. How many **speakers per recording** (average, range)?  
   b. Which **languages** are represented, and what is their distribution?  

4. **Annotations & Metadata**  
   a. What **annotation types** are provided (speaker turns, overlap labels, diarization error rates)?  
   b. What is the **annotation format** (RTTM, TextGrid, JSON, TSV)?  
   c. Is any **forced-alignment** or **transcript segmentation** available?  

5. **Licensing & Access**  
   a. What is the **licensing model** (open source, LDC license, data use agreement)?  
   b. What are the **download links** for audio, annotations, and documentation?  

6. **Data Splits & Benchmarks**  
   a. What **training/dev/test** splits are defined?  
   b. What **evaluation metrics** are recommended (DER, JER, CDER)?  
   c. Are there **baseline results** published (DER scores, system descriptions)?  

7. **Usage & Preprocessing**  
   a. What **preprocessing** steps are suggested (VAD, normalization, augmentation)?  
   b. Are there **existing toolkits** or **scripts** provided for data loading and preprocessing?  

8. **Challenges & Limitations**  
   a. What are the **known challenges** of the dataset (domain mismatch, overlap ratio, annotation errors)?  
   b. What **limitations** should be considered before training (license restrictions, missing segments)?  

9. **Research Applications**  
   a. Which **research tasks** is the dataset suited for (speaker embedding, diarization, overlap detection)?  
   b. Are there **notable papers** using this dataset and their key findings?  

10. **Reproducibility**  
   a. Can the dataset be **reproduced** (scripts, data generation)?  
   b. Are there **version controls** or **update logs** available?
